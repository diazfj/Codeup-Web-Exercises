¡Bienvenidos a Historic Market Square... one of the largest Mexican markets outside of Mexico!

Since the 1820's, Market Square has been a place for generations of residents and visitors to meet, shop, dine, and dance. Explore over 100 locally owned shops in a festive indoor mall filled with authentic Talavera pottery, exotic curios, handcrafted gifts, and colorful ethnic clothing. Savor traditional Mexican fare at popular establishments and surround yourself with the sounds of Mariachi and Tejano music. If that is not enough, nearly every weekend you can experience the rich cultural traditions of folklorico dancing on one of several stages throughout the marketplace. Historic Market Square is sure to have something for everyone!
